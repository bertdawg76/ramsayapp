Who we are & what we do

Lone Peak Home Inspection

SERVING SALT LAKE & UTAH COUNTY

I am a family man and love more than anything spending time with my wife and kids. I first got into construction when I was 15 years old doing landscaping. I have worked in several aspects of construction since then giving me a perfect platform for the home inspection business including masonry, landscaping, framing, electrical, HVAC, and solar. I am certified as a home inspector through InterNACHI, The International Association of Certified Home Inspectors. I am also a full time firefighter and as such it is very important that we know and understand the structure of a home. Not only do I pay extra close attention to potential fire hazards, I consider it my responsibility to to help you know exactly what you are getting when purchasing a home.
